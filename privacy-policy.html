Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-05-05

At Travelo, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app, including its features such as location-based recommendations for places, hotels and restaurants, favorites functionality, and user accounts.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@netrosystems.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and create a unique user ID for your account.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your precise location data to provide recommendations for nearby places, hotels, and restaurants.
  • User Preferences: Information about your favorite places, hotels, and restaurants that you save within the app.

1.2 Usage Data

  • App Usage: Data about how you use the app, such as the features you access, search queries, and interaction with recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Location-Based Services: We use your location data to suggest nearby places, hotels, and restaurants relevant to you.
  • Personalization: We use your favorites and preferences to customize your experience and provide more relevant recommendations.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, authenticate you when you log in, and maintain your favorite places.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app's functionality and user experience.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and process account deletion requests.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take your privacy seriously and do not share your personal information with third parties. All your data is stored securely and is only used to provide you with the services offered by the Travelo app.

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information only if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • Location Settings: You can enable or disable location services for Travelo at any time through your device settings. Please note that disabling location services will limit our ability to provide location-based recommendations.
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data by sending an email to support@netrosystems.com. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Favorites Management: You can add or remove favorites at any time through the app's interface.
  • Data Access and Correction: You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

7. Children's Privacy

Travelo is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@netrosystems.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you reside. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after such notifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
